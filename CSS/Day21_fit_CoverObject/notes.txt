fill: The content is stretched to completely fill the container box, potentially distorting its aspect ratio.by default property
contain: The content is scaled to maintain its aspect ratio while fitting within the container box. There may be empty space within the container.
cover: The content is scaled to cover the entire container, potentially cropping parts of the content to maintain aspect ratio.
none: The content is not resized. It retains its intrinsic size.
scale-down: The content is scaled to the smaller of none or contain. It will never be scaled up to be larger than its natural size.

                                object-position Property:
The object-position property defines the alignment of the replaced element's content within its container.
Values can be specified using keywords (left, center, right, top, bottom) or using length or percentage values to position the content relative to the container box.
